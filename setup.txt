3.) Create a new Discord Bot and activate all 3 Intents
4.) Go to OAUTH2, then to General and add the Webview Link without the '/' at the end as a Redirect
5.) Go to OAUTH2, then URL-Generator and scope 'identify' & 'guilds.join' (in this order)
6.) Copy the generated URL and save it.
7.) Fill out the Bot Token, Secret & ID at 'joiner.py' and 'main.py'
8.) Paste the Generated URL in 'main.py' at line 19
9.) Webhook isnt requiered
10.) Restart your Replit
11.) Put your tokens in 'tokens.txt' (on your computer)
12.) Run 'authorizer.py' and paste the generated URL
13.) Wait till all Tokens authorized and restart your Replit
14.) Run 'joiner.py' in Replit

15.) Enjoy! <3
